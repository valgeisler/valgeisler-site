The Activation Gap

Your sales team has their own deck. Your lifecycle emails don't drive product adoption, if they exist at all. And whatever materials someone built last quarter are sitting in a shared drive nobody opens.

That's not a messaging problem. That's an activation problem.

And it's costing you more than you think.

Prospects show up to sales calls and don't convert because they don't see their problem in what's being pitched.

Customers get onboarded to part of the product because nobody told the full story, so onboarding takes twice as long and churn goes up.

Partners are still selling the version of you from two years ago. Referrals fall flat because the people advocating for you don't have the language to do it well.

The wrong customers get in the door. The right ones don't see why it's them. And the people who should be sending you business don't know how to make the case.

This is the gap I close.
